TUTORIAL

The investment game is a two player, turn based game

Each turn, one player sends points to the other

Sending points represents doing somebody a favor,
      investing in a startup, or lending money to a friend

Each round of the game has four steps

$0 — Total A

Total B — $0

$0 — Available A

Available B — $0

A (Alfred)

B (Bubba)

Each game has five rounds

1. Alfred receives $10

2. Alfred sends some money to Bubba

3. Bubba receives 3X this amount

4. Buba sends some money back to Alfred

[5.] Update score and repeat

There is a tradeoff between immediate reward and long-term reputation

A (Alfred)

B (Bubba)

Generous — Alfred $15, Bubba $15

Greedy — Alfred $0, Bubba $30

If A and B are both generous, each will score moderately

If one player is greedy, they will score big at the expense of the other

There is no optimal strategy — you must learn through trial and error!

You will be randomly assigned to player A or B

Use the slider to choose an amount, then press send to submit

Wait while the other player makes a choice

A grayed out slider indicates their move

The website will record both players' moves and final scores
