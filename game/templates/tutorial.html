Welcome to the Investment Game Tutorial!

The Investment Game is a two-player game

The left player is the investor and the right player is the trustee

At the start of the game, you will be assigned to one of these players

Each game has 5 turns

On each turn, the investor and the trustee will each make one move

Let's go through an example turn, with you as the investor

First, the investor will get 10 coins

These coins represent money that the player may "invest" in the trustee or keep for themselves

Try it now!

Use the slider to select how many coins you will keep and how many you will give to the trustee

You'll notice that the trustee gets more coins than you're giving away ... press Send to learn more

When the investor gives away coins, the trustee always gets 3 times that many coins!

Figuratively, the trustee uses the investor's gift as "startup money" to (somehow) make a large profit

It is now the trustee's' turn...

The trustee will give back some of their coins to the investor and keep the rest

The turn is now over

Coins collected by the investor and trustee are added to their respective scores

... and the next turn begins!

As your score increases past hidden thresholds (e.g. 60 coins), you will unlock bonus rewards

To add these rewards to your real-money winnings, you must finish the game
