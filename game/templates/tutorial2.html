In the Investment Game, there is a tradeoff between immediate reward and long-term reputation

To demonstrate this, we'll play two mini-games (2 turns each)

In the first game, you'll be the investor and we'll force you to make generous investments

In the second game, you'll be the trustee and we'll for you to make greedy returns

You've just finished your first game!

By investing generously, both you and the trustee have collected a good number of coins

Your final score is X and the instructor's score is Y

If, instead, you had chosen to keep everything and invest nothing, the final scores would have been 20 and 0

This shows that investing generously can benefit both players ... but what if the trustee gets greedy?

In this game, you'll play as the trustee

We'll force you to make greedy decisions, and see what happens ...

When the trustee is greedy, they can collect many coins on a single turn

However, this action may anger the investor, causing them to give less in the future

Check the Game Log at the bottom to see this for yourself

There is no optimal strategy in the Investment Game — you must learn the best way to deal with each opponent through trial and error!

When playing games, you will face a variety of opponents without knowing their identity. Your opponent might be:

a dumb agent (a computer that always make greedy or generous moves)
a smart agent (a computer that responds to your choices and learns over time)
another human player

Our research study is focused on how you learn over time, so try your best, and earn bonus rewards for playing well!

To complete the study, you must complete 10 games. View your progress in the upper left.

You may also play up to 30 bonus games to earn more rewards , at a rate of $0.05-$0.25 per game

Receive your HIT code and bonus payment by selecting Cash Out from the upper right

You must complete the Survey for Cash Out to become available. The Survey can be accessed through the Home page.

You have completed the tutorial and may now start playing games. Happy investing!
